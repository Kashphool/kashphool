## Idea 1: "Sacred Geometry Modernism"

**Design Movement:** Neo-Traditional Indian Minimalism — merging sacred geometric patterns from Hindu temple architecture with Swiss-style modernism.

**Core Principles:**
1. Sacred geometry as structural DNA — mandalas, lotus patterns, and yantra-inspired grids inform layout divisions
2. Restrained opulence — rich materials (gold leaf textures, silk-like gradients) used sparingly against vast breathing space
3. Vertical rhythm inspired by temple gopurams — content stacks upward with increasing visual weight
4. Dualistic contrast — dark charcoal grounds paired with luminous saffron and gold accents

**Color Philosophy:** A palette rooted in temple interiors — deep charcoal (#1a1a2e) as the primary ground representing the cosmic void, saffron (#e8a838) as the sacred fire, vermillion (#c0392b) as sindoor/kumkum, antique gold (#d4a843) for divinity, and warm ivory (#faf3e0) for purity. The emotional intent is reverence meeting warmth.

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric split-panel layouts where content and imagery occupy unequal halves, connected by decorative geometric dividers. Sections flow vertically with alternating emphasis (left-heavy, then right-heavy). No centered hero — instead, a dramatic diagonal split.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Animated mandala/lotus SVG borders that subtly rotate on scroll
2. Gold-foil accent lines that trace section boundaries like temple carvings
3. Alpona (Bengali floor art) patterns as section backgrounds at low opacity

**Interaction Philosophy:** Interactions feel like offerings — hover states bloom like lotus petals, clicks ripple outward like water in a temple pond, scroll reveals content like unrolling a sacred scroll.

**Animation:** Sections fade-and-rise on scroll with staggered timing. Gold accent lines draw themselves in as they enter viewport. Gallery images reveal with a curtain-pull effect. The ॐ symbol in the header gently pulses with a breathing rhythm.

**Typography System:** Display: "Playfair Display" (serif, ornate) for headings — conveys tradition and elegance. Body: "Source Sans 3" (clean sans-serif) for readability. Bengali/Hindi text uses "Noto Sans Bengali". Hierarchy: H1 at 4rem bold, H2 at 2.5rem semibold, body at 1.125rem regular.

## Idea 2: "Festive Terracotta Warmth"

**Design Movement:** Bengali Folk Art Revival — inspired by Kalighat paintings, terracotta temple panels, and Durga Puja pandal aesthetics translated into digital form.

**Core Principles:**
1. Hand-crafted authenticity — every element should feel like it was made by an artisan, not a machine
2. Earthy warmth — terracotta, turmeric, and clay tones dominate, grounding the digital in the physical
3. Layered storytelling — content reveals itself in layers like peeling back pandal decorations
4. Celebratory energy — the site should feel like walking into a puja pandal during Durga Puja

**Color Philosophy:** Drawn from Bengal's earth and festivals — terracotta red (#c44536) as the foundation color of clay temples, turmeric yellow (#e8b04b) for auspiciousness, deep forest green (#2d5016) for banana leaves and mango leaves used in decoration, cream white (#f5f0e8) as the dhoti/sari white, and burnt sienna (#a0522d) for depth. Emotional intent: joyful celebration grounded in earthy tradition.

**Layout Paradigm:** Overlapping card-based layout where sections slightly overlap each other vertically, creating depth like stacked pandal panels. Content cards have slightly irregular edges (CSS clip-path) mimicking hand-cut paper or clay tiles. Full-width sections alternate with contained asymmetric grids.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Terracotta-textured section dividers shaped like temple arches
2. Decorative borders inspired by Kantha stitch patterns (dashed, hand-drawn feel)
3. Floating marigold garland illustrations connecting sections

**Interaction Philosophy:** Interactions feel festive and tactile — hover states add a warm glow like oil lamp light, buttons have a pressed-clay depth effect, scrolling triggers gentle particle effects of flower petals.

**Animation:** Content enters with a gentle swing animation (like hanging decorations). Gallery images have a polaroid-shuffle reveal. Event cards slide in from alternating sides. Background has subtle floating particle effects (marigold petals, light sparkles).

**Typography System:** Display: "Yeseva One" (decorative serif) for headings — evokes hand-painted signage. Body: "Nunito" (rounded sans-serif) for warmth and approachability. Hierarchy: H1 at 3.5rem, H2 at 2.25rem, body at 1rem. Letter-spacing slightly loose on headings for a relaxed, festive feel.

## Idea 3: "Luminous Silk & Incense"

**Design Movement:** Contemporary Indian Luxe — inspired by high-end Indian fashion editorials, Banarasi silk textures, and the atmospheric quality of incense smoke in dimly lit temples.

**Core Principles:**
1. Atmospheric depth — layered translucency and blur effects create a sense of sacred space
2. Silk-like fluidity — smooth gradients and flowing shapes evoke draped fabric
3. Cinematic framing — each section is composed like a film still, with dramatic lighting
4. Intimate scale — content feels close and personal, like a whispered prayer

**Color Philosophy:** Inspired by evening aarti — deep navy (#0f1b2d) as twilight sky, rich burgundy (#6b1d3a) as silk, warm amber (#d4a843) as flame light, soft rose (#e8c4b8) as skin tone warmth, and pearl white (#f8f4ef) for highlights. The emotional intent is intimate devotion and quiet grandeur.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-viewport sections with cinematic aspect ratios. Content is positioned using a rule-of-thirds grid (not centered). Large atmospheric background images with content overlaid in floating glass-morphism panels. Horizontal scroll for the gallery section.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Glass-morphism content panels with backdrop-blur creating an incense-smoke effect
2. Gradient mesh backgrounds that shift subtly on mouse movement (parallax)
3. Thin gold filigree lines as decorative accents between content blocks

**Interaction Philosophy:** Interactions feel ethereal — hover states create a soft glow like candlelight, transitions are slow and graceful like silk falling, content fades in like emerging from incense smoke.

**Animation:** Slow, cinematic fade-ins (800ms+) with slight scale transforms. Parallax depth on background layers. Gallery uses a smooth horizontal scroll with momentum. Gold filigree lines animate as continuous flowing paths. Subtle grain texture overlay for film-like quality.

**Typography System:** Display: "Cormorant Garamond" (elegant serif) for headings — refined and editorial. Body: "Lato" (clean sans-serif) for modern readability. Hierarchy: H1 at 4.5rem light weight (ethereal feel), H2 at 2.75rem medium, body at 1.05rem. Generous line-height (1.8) for breathing room.
